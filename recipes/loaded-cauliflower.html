Loaded Cauliflower

An easy and delicious low carb cauliflower dish for dairy lovers.

Ingredients

  • Whole head of cauliflower
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • Homemade ranch seasoning (1 tsp dried parsley, 1 tsp seasoned salt, 3/4 tsp ground pepper, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, 1/4 tsp onion powder, 1/8 tsp dried thyme)
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp butter, cut into small pieces

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Fill pot with water and bring it to a boil, and then place cauliflower in a steamer basket and steam for 10-15 minutes
  3. Mix cauliflower, sour cream, homemade ranch seasoning, onion and garlic powder in a 9 inch baking dish and top with butter and cheddar cheese
  4. Bake in oven for 30-45 minutes until bubbly and cheese is melted
  5. Serve and enjoy!
