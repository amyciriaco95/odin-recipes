Zucchini Lasagna

None of the carbs, but all of the goodness.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 large zucchini
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 pound of mushrooms, rinsed and wiped
  • 1 1/2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 3 cloves of garlic, sliced
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 (8 oz) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3 tbsp of nutritional yeast

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (~ 200 degrees C)
  2. Thinly slice zucchini into slices, salt and use paper towel to remove excess moisture
  3. Grease two large cookie sheets with 1 tbsp of olive oil and lay slices of zucchini on sheets
  4. Bake zucchini slices in oven for 6-8 minutes per side, and then flip and bake for an additional 6-8 minutes. Set aside.
  5. Cut mushrooms and garlic into small pieces and put them in a saute pan
  6. Add olive oil to mushroom and garlic and saute until softened
  7. Add 1 cup of marinara sauce and dried basil to mushroom and garlic
  8. Stir and simmer until sauce thickens
  9. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, mozzarella cheese and nutritional yeast
  10. Spread 1/2 cup of marinara sauce to bottom of lasagna pan
  11. Add slices of zucchini
  12. Spread ricotta mixture on top along with the marinara and mushroom mixture
  13. Repeat layers and top with remaining mozzarella and nutritional yeast
  14. Bake covered for 30 minutes and 10 minutes uncovered
  15. Allow to cool and enjoy!
